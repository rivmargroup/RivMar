The Process

Digital Precision, Flawless Fit

We use advanced photogrammetry to map your vessel with millimeter accuracy — no paper templates, no guesswork.

📸

Digital Capture

Using advanced PhotoModeler technology, we digitally map the exact contours of your vessel's deck, replacing rudimentary paper templates with millimeter-accurate 3D data.

🛠

Precision Design

The raw digital data is imported into our CAD systems where our engineers design the custom layout, ensuring perfect alignment with hardware, hatches, and curves.

✔

Expert Installation

CNC-routed to exact specifications, the finished product is installed by our seasoned technicians for a flawless, seamless aesthetic that looks factory-original.
